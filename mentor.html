Find a Mentor

Whether you're just starting your flight training or transitioning into a new specialty, connecting with experienced aviators can guide your journey. These are some trusted mentoring communities.

Whirly-Girls International

A global organization of female helicopter pilots offering scholarships, mentoring, and networking.

Women in Aviation International

Supporting women across all aviation fields through events, mentorship, and resources.

The Ninety-Nines

An international organization of women pilots founded in 1929, dedicated to advancement through education and mentoring.

NGPA

National Gay Pilots Association fosters equality and mentorship for LGBTQ+ pilots and aviation professionals.

OBAP

Organization of Black Aerospace Professionals connect underrepresented youth and professionals in aerospace through mentorship, training, and scholarships.

AeroEducate

An outreach program by the EAA to inspire and educate youth through hands-on aviation experiences.
