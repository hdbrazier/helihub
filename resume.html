General Pilot Resume

A flexible format for pilots with mixed or unconventional experience — adaptable for contract work, government agencies, or career changes. In your pilot resume, discuss your experience and skills relevant to aviation. Showcase flight hours, types of aircraft flown, and any notable missions or flights to demonstrate your experience in a tangible way.

Commercial Pilot Resume

Tailored for commercial helicopter pilots, this resume highlights flight time, aircraft types, and mission logs that matter to employers like charter companies or EMS providers.

Flight Instructor Resume

This template emphasizes teaching experience, FAA certifications, and student success metrics — ideal for pilots transitioning into or advancing in instructional roles.

Student Pilot Resume

Perfect for students applying to flight schools or internships. Focuses on education, early achievements, and passion for aviation. Including hobbies is a powerful way to demonstrate your potential at a personal and professional level. For instance, traveling can demonstrate your enthusiasm for the industry.

Resume Tips for Helicopter Pilots

  • Highlight total flight hours and certifications.
  • List specialized training (e.g., night vision, turbine engines).
  • Include specific mission types (EMS, utility, tour, offshore, etc.).
  • Keep it to one page unless highly experienced.
